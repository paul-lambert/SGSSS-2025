This folder features data files used within lab exercises at the training session:

 Multilevel Models for Applied Social Research
 Scottish Graduate School of Social Sciences, Summer School 2025


There are no restrictions on use of those files which constitute open data within these files, comprising: 

gcse.dta, nurses.dta, popular2.dta, pupcross.dta, censusmicroteaching11s.dta, censusmicroteaching11s.dta


All other files within this folder are anonymised extracts from existing surveys and should only be 
used for the purposes of study within the training workshop at the SGSSS summer school. They should 
not be redistributed or used for other purposes. A password is given to workshop participants to open 
this files from the zip archive.
